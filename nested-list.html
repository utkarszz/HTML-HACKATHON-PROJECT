NESTED LIST

A nested list in HTML refers to a list that is placed inside another list, creating a hierarchical structure. This allows for the organization of information into subcategories or subtopics, providing a clear visual representation of relationships between items.

CHALO EXAMPLE DEKHTE HAII

  • Fruits
    • Apple
    • Banana
  • Vegetables
    • Carrot
    • Broccoli
